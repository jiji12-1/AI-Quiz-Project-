AI Hallucination Quiz
An interactive web-based quiz designed to demonstrate how easily AI systems can produce confident but incorrect information. Users must distinguish real AI-generated answers from hallucinations, then view educational content explaining why hallucinations happen and how to think critically about AI outputs.
